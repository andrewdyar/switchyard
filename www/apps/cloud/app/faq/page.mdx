# {metadata.title}

In this guide, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions about Cloud.

## Do I need to learn about Switchyard to use Cloud?

Since you deploy Switchyard projects on Cloud, you need a developer who is familiar with Switchyard to set up your project and add customizations for your business needs. Learn about Switchyard in the [Getting Started](!docs!/learn) guide.

You can also contact a [Switchyard Expert](https://switchyard.com/experts/) to help you with building your Switchyard project and deploying it on Cloud.

---

## Does Switchyard support hosting storefronts on Cloud?

Switchyard can only host Switchyard servers and admin dashboards. To host your storefront, you can use hosting providers like Vercel.

Learn more in the [Connect Production Storefront](../connect-storefront/page.mdx) guide.

---

## What resources does Switchyard provide in Cloud?

Switchyard fully-manages your [PostgreSQL databases](../database/page.mdx), [Redis instances](../redis/page.mdx), and [S3 buckets](../s3/page.mdx) on Cloud. You don't need to configure or maintain them.

---

## Do I own my code and data for my Switchyard project on Cloud?

Yes, your code is hosted on your GitHub repository. You can also export a dump of your Cloud database at any time.

---

## Can I deploy multiple projects on Cloud?

Yes, if your plan supports multiple projects. Learn more in the [Plans & Pricing](../pricing/page.mdx) guide.

---

## Can I use my custom plugins and modules on Cloud?

Yes, you can use any custom and out-of-the-box plugins and modules in your Switchyard project deployed on Cloud. You retain the same flexibility as you would with a self-hosted Switchyard project.

You can deploy projects like marketplaces, B2B, subscription-based stores, and more on Cloud.

---

## Does Switchyard create new Switchyard projects for each deployment on Cloud?

No, Switchyard does not create new Switchyard projects for your deployments. Instead, it deploys your existing Switchyard project from your GitHub repository.

---

## Can I use a static IP address for my Switchyard project on Cloud?

No, Switchyard does not provide a static IP address for your Switchyard project on Cloud. However, you can use a proxy that has a static IP address to connect to your Switchyard project.

Contact support for help with setting up a proxy for your Switchyard project.

---

## My card from India is not being accepted. What should I do?

Cards issued by banks in India are not supported. Please use cards issued by banks in other countries.

You can also [contact support](https://switchyard.com/contact/) to discuss alternative payment methods for Pro and Enterprise plans.
